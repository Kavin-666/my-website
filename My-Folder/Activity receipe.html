🍦 Homemade Ice Cream Recipe

A simple, creamy treat to enjoy anytime!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the sugar and milk until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Stir in the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Pour the mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  4. Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.
  5. Enjoy your creamy homemade ice cream!
